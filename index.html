General Class information

All adult and youth classes take place at Ballet Co. Laboratory (276 E Lafayette Frontage Road, Saint Paul, MN 55107).

I believe in affordable dance education, so additional uniforms/costumes or supplies are not needed. All dancers should wear close fitting apparel such as leggings/yoga pants/shorts or slim fit items and non-baggy tops (this allows students to see their body lines to learn better and keeps them safe while dancing). Students should also plan to be bare footed, however if shoes are desired due to feet sensitivity or for extra support, please reach out to me and I can recommend a couple cost effective options and where to get them. Please also bring a full water bottle.

Bio

Hi, I’m Bethany, and I am so glad you are here!

I live in Roseville with my husband and our dog Piper, who is a husky and golden retriever mix. By day I work at a church in White Bear Lake as a youth director for kids ages 3-18 and in the evening, I like to be involved with dance or theatre, hang with friends, go on an adventure or enjoy a night in.

I have trained in modern, contemporary, jazz, tap, musical theatre, and hip hop dance styles not only as an adolescent, but also in my adult career at Moorhead State University, Concordia University and Zenon Dance. During my time at Concordia, I was encouraged to take a choreography class by my dance professor, and it opened a whole new world to me of creating and teaching. I have had 8 original choreographed pieces produced and have also choreographed for a couple children’s theaters in the Twin Cities area.

The instruction and care shown to me by my dance teachers has shaped part of who I am today, kept my love for dance strong and has shown me what sharing your passion with students looks like. My style of teaching is rooted in a solid foundation of dance basics, as that is where all movement begins. However, it is not where it stops, and so I share with students that there is an endless world of possibilities when it comes to movement.

Students learn not only the core basics that will shape them into a well experienced dancer, but that dance is truly an expression of story, experience and often what cannot be put into words. Beyond that, I have a passion for showing every person that I meet that they are loved, valued and important, and that shapes the way I teach as well. I hope to meet you soon!
